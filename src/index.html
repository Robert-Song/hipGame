About the game

Hip is a game described by Martin Gardner in (find exact source). Though his column asks only about the possibility of the game coming to a draw as a sort of logic exercise, it's playable and enjoyable as any other tabletop game, with the difficulty of having to check your - and the other player's - every move for any of numerous squares. The online game remedies this, allowing players to practice both recognizing squares and playing a more elaborate strategy against a simple AI.
